hello all

gravo is a 26 year old man that was born in the capitol of iceland, reykjavík.
he was forced to be relocated to the far east of iceland to a small town called
seyðisfjörður at the age of only 9 months old.

gravo was raised by his grandparents who a few months later in the year after,
had their own son - his father's half brother. despite that they were practically
raised as twins for the first years of their lives.

gravo had a normal childhood for the most part, except for the whole relocation part.
gravo was lucky, in that he was allowed to have a relationship with his parents.
gravo often took a planeride over to the capitol to meet and spend time with them.
his parents also frequently visited him in seyðisfjörður.

gravo has a few diagnoses, adhd, tourettes and anxiety.
today, gravo is happy, in love and thrives not inspite of his conditions, but because of them.
